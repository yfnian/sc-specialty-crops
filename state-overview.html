1.1.1 Trend of Production Acreage

Figure 1.1 shows an overview of specialty crop production acreage across different types of crops over the years. Total production acreage has increased over time. Vegetables, melons, and potatoes account for the largest share, making up more than 50% of the total specialty crop acreage. The second-largest category is non-citrus fruit production, followed by tree nuts in third place and berries in fourth place. Since 2007, citrus fruit production acreage has remained minimal, reaching only 11 acres in 2022.
